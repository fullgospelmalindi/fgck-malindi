Our Ministries

At Full Gospel Churches of Kenya – Malindi, we have different ministries that help believers grow spiritually, serve the community, and strengthen their relationship with God.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry focuses on guiding young people spiritually and helping them grow in faith. Activities include Bible study, worship, mentorship, and youth conferences.

Children Ministry

The Children Ministry teaches young children the Word of God in a fun and interactive way through Bible stories, songs, and activities during the children's service.

Women Ministry

The Women Ministry supports and empowers women spiritually through prayer meetings, fellowship, and teaching programs that strengthen faith and family life.

Men Ministry

The Men Ministry brings together men of the church for spiritual growth, leadership development, and fellowship through Bible studies and prayer meetings.

Worship Ministry

The Worship Ministry leads the congregation in praise and worship during church services. This ministry includes singers, instrumentalists, and technical support teams.
