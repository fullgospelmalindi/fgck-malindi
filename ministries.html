Church Ministries

Our church has several ministries that help believers grow spiritually, serve others, and reach the community with the love of Christ.

Children Ministry

This ministry teaches children the Word of God and helps them grow in faith through Bible lessons, worship, and activities designed for their age.

Youth Ministry

The youth ministry nurtures young people spiritually, emotionally, and socially while encouraging them to live a Christ-centered life.

Women Ministry

This ministry empowers women through fellowship, prayer, mentorship, and teachings that strengthen their faith and role in the family and church.

Men Ministry

The men's ministry helps men grow in leadership, responsibility, and faith while supporting families and the church community.

Ushering / Protocol Ministry

This ministry welcomes visitors and members, maintains order during services, and assists in the smooth running of church events.

Media Ministry

The media ministry manages church sound, projection, photography, and online communication to spread the Gospel both physically and digitally.

Intercessory Ministry

This ministry focuses on prayer and spiritual warfare, interceding for the church, the nation, and the community.

Sports Department

The sports ministry connects youth and members through sports activities while promoting unity and healthy living.

Mercy Ministries

This ministry focuses on outreach to the needy, supporting widows, orphans, and vulnerable members of the community through acts of compassion.
