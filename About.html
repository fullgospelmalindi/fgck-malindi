About Full Gospel Churches of Kenya

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya (FGCK) is one of the largest Pentecostal Christian denominations in Kenya. The church is dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers, and transforming communities through the power of the Word of God and the Holy Spirit.

History of the Church

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya was founded in 1949 in Kisumu District by missionaries from Finland. These missionaries worked together with Kenyan believers to establish the first congregations and spread Christianity in different parts of the country. Over time, the church expanded rapidly and now has hundreds of assemblies across Kenya.

Vision

The vision of Full Gospel Churches of Kenya is to be a dynamic church of God-fearing believers who know Jesus Christ intimately and reach out to all people with the Gospel.

Mission

  • Preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • Making disciples of believers.
  • Establishing strong local church assemblies.
  • Equipping believers spiritually through teaching and prayer.
  • Serving communities through social and development programs.

Our Beliefs

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya follows Pentecostal Christian teachings that emphasize faith in the Triune God (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit), the authority of the Bible as the Word of God, salvation through Jesus Christ, and the power of the Holy Spirit in the life of every believer.

FGCK Malindi

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya – Malindi is committed to preaching the Gospel, discipling believers, and transforming lives through worship, prayer, and the teaching of God's Word. The church welcomes people from all backgrounds to experience spiritual growth and fellowship in Christ.
